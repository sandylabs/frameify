Your Privacy, Our Top Priority

At Frameify, we uphold your privacy. We're designed from the ground up to operate without collecting any personal data.

This policy applies to Frameify. Last updated: August 2025

Our Core Privacy Philosophy

We believe privacy is not a feature, but a fundamental right. This is why Frameify is built to work completely without knowing who you are.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear and transparent. Frameify does not collect your personal information in any form.

No Account, No Sign-Up

You don't need to create an account, log in with an email, or provide any personal details to use Frameify. Our service is completely anonymous.

No Usage Data Tracking

We don't track which features you use, how long you use the app, or your IP address. Your privacy is fully protected.

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use cookies to track you across the web or collect any information about your Browse habits.

No Access to Your Media

We do not access your gallery or media data. Everything you upload is for temporary use and is processed on your device.

2. How Your Creations Are Handled

We ensure that your photos and creations are handled with utmost security, because everything happens locally.

Local Processing on Your Device

All creative processes, from uploading photos from your gallery to adding frames, filters, and stickers, are done entirely in your browser. We never receive, store, or view your photo data. Once you download your final creation, all temporary data is automatically deleted from your browser.

3. Data Security & Sharing

Because we don't collect data, there's no risk of data breaches or sharing.

No Data to Share

We have no user database. Since we don't collect any of your personal information, we have nothing to share, sell, or disclose to any third parties.

Privacy by Design

The architecture of Frameify is built with privacy as a core principle. By not collecting any data, privacy risks are inherently eliminated.

4. Local Storage and Temporary Data

We use your browser's local storage for seamless functionality. This data is fully controlled by you.

Preference Storage

Frameify may store your preferences (e.g., last layout choice or filter settings) in your browser's local storage. This is done to make your experience smoother when you return to the app. This data is never sent to our servers. It's like a small note saved on your own device.

Temporary Photo Processing

When you upload or take photos, they are stored temporarily in your browser's memory for processing. Once you download the final result, these photos are automatically removed. This ensures that no digital traces are left on your device after the session is complete.

Temporary Session Data

To maintain an optimal user experience, Frameify may use local storage to save temporary session data, such as the progress of the creation you are working on. This helps prevent the loss of your work if you accidentally close the tab. This data is temporary and will be lost once you finish.

Local Customization

All customizations you make, including adding stickers and effects, are managed entirely on your device. Our servers are never involved in this process.

Non-Personal Technical Information

If any, we may collect anonymous technical data about browser type and app version to ensure compatibility and stability. This data cannot personally identify you.

Local Security

The protection of your on-device data is our priority. All temporary data is encrypted and isolated within your browser, ensuring no unauthorized access.

No Third Parties

We do not use third-party services that could track or collect your data. The entire Frameify experience is self-contained.

Sensitive Information

Since we do not collect data, we have no sensitive information about you. You can use our service with complete peace of mind.

Openness and Transparency

We are committed to always being transparent. If this policy ever changes, we will be completely open and clear about it on this page.

User Experience

All our decisions are based on enhancing user experience, not on data collection.

Privacy Continuity

We pledge to maintain your privacy as our highest priority in the future.

Simple Privacy Policy

We ensure our privacy policy is easy to understand and free of legal jargon.

No Advertisements

Without data, there are no targeted advertisements or unwanted promotions.

Law and Regulations

We comply with all applicable privacy laws and always strive to exceed those standards.

Contact Us

If you have any further questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13, and since no data is collected, this provides an extra layer of protection.

Policy Updates

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

No Biometric Data Collection

We do not collect any biometric data from your photos.

Clean Code

Our code is built with good intentions and does not include any hidden functions to collect your data.

Data Ownership

You are the sole owner of your creations and photos.

No Server Logs

Our servers do not store logs that can identify you.

Browser Security

The security of your data depends on your browser's security, as that's where the data is stored.

No Marketing

Without data, there are no targeted marketing or unwanted promotions.

Serverless System

We are designed to be a largely serverless system, which significantly reduces privacy risks.
